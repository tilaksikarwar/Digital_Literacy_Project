Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

1. Use strong and unique passwords for all accounts and enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible.

2. Never share your OTP, passwords, or banking details with anyone, even if they claim to be from a bank or official service.

3. Be cautious of suspicious links, emails, or messages, especially those asking for urgent action or personal information.

4. Always verify the authenticity of websites before entering sensitive information (check HTTPS and correct URL).

5. Do not accept friend requests or messages from unknown people on social media platforms.

6. Avoid sharing personal information such as address, phone number, or college details publicly online.

7. For UPI safety: Double-check the receiver’s name and UPI ID before making any payment.

8. For UPI safety: Never approve “collect requests” or payment requests from unknown sources.

9. Keep your devices updated with the latest security patches and use antivirus software.

10. If you face any cybercrime, report it immediately on the National Cyber Crime Portal (https://cybercrime.gov.in) or call the helpline number 1930.
