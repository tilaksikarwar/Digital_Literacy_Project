Social Media Do's and Don'ts Checklist

DO's:
1. Use strong and unique passwords for all your accounts.
2. Keep your profiles professional and appropriate.
3. Verify information before sharing it online.
4. Respect others' opinions and communicate politely.
5. Adjust privacy settings to control who can see your content.

DON'Ts:
1. Do not share personal information like phone number, address, or passwords.
2. Do not post offensive, harmful, or inappropriate content.
3. Do not click on suspicious links or unknown attachments.
4. Do not accept friend requests from unknown people.
5. Do not engage in cyberbullying or online arguments.
